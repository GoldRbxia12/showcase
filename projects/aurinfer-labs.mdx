# AurInfer Labs

## Overview

AurInfer Labs is an AI platform that lets you create your own AI personalities as characters or interact with those built by the community. You can integrate them with platforms like Discord, X, and Bluesky, or chat directly on the web. It also supports voice features, including voice messages and real-time voice interaction in voice channels.

## Key Features

- AI Personality Creation: Easily create your own custom AI personalities as characters. Tailor their behavior, dialogue, and style, or choose from a growing community of pre-built characters.

- Voice Integration: Add voice capabilities to your AI characters, including voice messages and real-time voice interaction within voice channels (e.g., Discord). Bring your characters to life with audio.

- Multi-Platform Integration: Seamlessly integrate your AI characters across various platforms like Discord, X (formerly Twitter), Bluesky, and more. Engage with your AI or the community on your favorite platform.
